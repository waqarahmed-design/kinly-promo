# Health Tracker Promo — DESIGN.md

## Style Prompt
Deep royal purple on dark canvas. Trust-heavy, premium, warm. The primary user (Fatima) is a family caregiver who is overwhelmed, not technical. The visual tone should feel calm and authoritative — like a product that has its act together when the user doesn't. Not cold tech, not playful wellness. Premium dark with lavender warmth.

## Colors
- Background: `#0D0B1A` — very deep purple-black (app brand.900 + slightly lighter)
- Brand primary: `#221080` — deep royal purple (exact match from app theme)
- Accent glow: `#6C5CE7` — brighter purple for video luminance (derived from brand)
- Text primary: `#FFFFFF`
- Text secondary: `#C4C2F0` — pale lavender (app brand.200)
- Ghost/decorative: `rgba(196,194,240,0.05)` — near-invisible brand tint

## Typography
- Display: Instrument Serif — trust, editorial warmth, not cold
- Body / UI: Plus Jakarta Sans — modern, clean, legible at video scale

## Motion Character
- Calm authority. Not explosive. Not playful.
- Entrances: 0.4–0.7s. Exits: handled by transitions only.
- Easing variety: expo.out (confident), power3.out (smooth), back.out (warmth), sine.inOut (ambient)
- Ambient: breathing glows, slow drifting ghost text. One ambient motion per scene, not five.

## What NOT to Do
- No green — this is not a fitness tracker
- No cyan/teal/blue gradients — the brand is purple, not tech
- No centered-stack layout for every scene — use left-anchor and zone-based layouts
- No cold, clinical visual language — Fatima is a mother, not a doctor
- No generic health emoji or stock UI screenshots
